81 - Kabali is a brave warrior who with his group of young ninjas moves from one place to another to fight against his opponents. Before Fighting he just calculates one thing, the difference between his ninja number and the opponent's ninja number. From this difference he decides whether to fight or
not. Kabali's ninja number is never greater than his opponent.
Input
The input contains two numbers in every line. These two numbers in each line denotes the number ninjas in Kabali's clan and his opponent's clan or vice versa. The input numbers are not greater than 2^32. Input is terminated by `End of File'. 
Output
For each line of input, print the difference of number of ninjas between Kabali's clan and his opponent's clan. Each output should be in seperate line.
Sample Testcase :
INPUT
10 12
10 14
100 200
OUTPUT
2
4
100

Input  is  a  sequence  of  lines. Each  line  will contain an input number. One or more spaces. A  sign  ( division or  mod). Again  spaces. And  another  input  number.  Both the input numbers are non-negative  integer.

1
9
1
2147483646
